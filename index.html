How to be entered into the raffle:
1. Your invited friend must make it through the application process.
2. Your invited friend must specify to Sneefli that they were invited by you. This can be during applications or after by DMing Sneefli directly.
3. You can invite multiple people and be entered into the raffle up to five (5) times.
4. People you invite can also participate in the recruit-a-friend event by inviting their friends (refer to 4).
5. This event is designed to help grow our community and reward you for helping. We kindly ask that you not invite people you think wouldn't be interested in Polaris.

x. If caught inviting fake accounts, you will be suspended from the server in entirety.

How the prize is awarded:
Participants are entered into the raffle as duos. Both you and a friend will win one (1) month of Discord Nitro Classic each if selected. This raffle is completely random, but you increase your chances of winning by inviting multiple people. You as an individual can only win once. If two duos you're a part of win, you'll each receive one (1) month of Discord Nitro Classic each and the remaining nitro classic will go to a random participant in the event.

The winners will be pulled and announced on 7/10/2021. Three (3) duos will win Nitro Classic, adding up to six (6) total months of Discord Nitro being given away. We will have a week from 7/10/2021 to award you your Nitro Classic.
